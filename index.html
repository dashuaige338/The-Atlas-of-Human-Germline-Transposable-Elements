About the hPGC visualizer

Welcome to The hPGC visualizer

version 0.1

The hPGC visualizer was created based on previous bulk RNA-seq dataset and single cell RNA-seq dataset with the purpose of enabling a broad audience to explore the gene and transposable element (TE) expression profiles of human germline and somatic cell development at bulk level or at a single cell resolution. At this portal, visitors can freely use the two provided datasets to interactively explore how male and female human germline cells undergo transcriptional changes during prenatal life in parallel with their surrounding somatic cells.
